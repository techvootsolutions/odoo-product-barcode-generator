Product Barcode Generator for Odoo

Struggling with manual barcode entry, duplicate codes, or untagged products? Techvoot's Product Barcode Generator for Odoo automates unique barcode creation for every product individually or in bulk saving hours of manual work and eliminating inventory errors.

Odoo does not auto-generate product barcodes by default leaving businesses to assign them manually, risking errors and duplicates. This module fills that gap by providing a powerful, configurable barcode generation engine directly inside Odoo. Whether you manage 10 products or 10,000, it works seamlessly for both Community and Enterprise versions, including Odoo.sh.

Key Features

Everything your team needs to manage product barcodes in Odoo without third-party tools or manual spreadsheets.

Auto-Generate Unique Barcodes

Instantly assign a unique barcode to any product with one click no manual entry, no duplicates.

Configurable Formats & Sequences

Set prefix rules, sequence patterns, and barcode format (Code128, EAN13, etc.) to match your workflow.

Bulk Barcode Generation

Select hundreds of products at once and generate or update all their barcodes in a single action.

Replace or Retain Existing Barcodes

Why Businesses Choose This Odoo Barcode Module

Manually creating barcodes for hundreds of products is time-consuming and error-prone. Techvoot's Product Barcode Generator is built specifically for Odoo users who need a reliable, scalable, and easy-to-use barcode automation solution. It integrates natively with Odoo's inventory, product catalog, and point-of-sale modules so there's no duplicate data entry and no third-party barcode tools to manage.

Compatible with Odoo Community, Enterprise, and Odoo.sh, the module supports multiple barcode formats including Code128, EAN13, EAN8, Code39, and more. Whether you're onboarding a new product catalog or standardizing barcodes across an existing inventory, this module makes the entire process fast, accurate, and fully automated. Trusted by businesses across retail, manufacturing, and warehousing.
